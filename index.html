About Quiz Forge

Quiz Forge is a platform that has been designed on the foundation of creating a revision tool for mastering concepts through interactive quizzes and game board. Whether you're a student preparing for exams, a teacher wanting to create a fun way of setting questions for students or someone who is just after a bit of fun, Quiz Forge provides a dynamic learning experience tailored to your needs.

What We Offer

  • Focused Programming Questions: Quiz Forge has been set up with questions to test you knowledge on HTML, CSS and Javascript.
  • Beyond Programming: While programming is our primary focus, our importable question option allows for users to customise their learning based on their needs

How it Works

Simple hit to take you to the first question. You can select through the game by clicking on the folder icons, or using your keyboard.

Importing your own questions

Simply upload your questions to the form in the menu and save your questions to the browser. Important note, if you clear your browser cache, you will lose any stored customised questions.
